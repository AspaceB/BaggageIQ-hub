2. What BaggageIQ does

BaggageIQ is a browser extension that reads flight search results on Skyscanner, Google Flights, Kayak, MakeMyTrip and CheapOAir and overlays an estimated baggage surcharge next to each flight, using a database of airline baggage policies maintained by the project. The goal is to help you compare the real total cost of flights before you book.

The free tier is always free. BaggageIQ Pro is an optional paid add-on that unlocks credit-card baggage benefits (see Pricing).

3. Accuracy of the estimates — important

Read this carefully. BaggageIQ fee and card-benefit estimates are approximations based on publicly available airline and card-issuer policies. They are not quotes, bookings, or guarantees.

Airlines change their fees, bag allowances, and fare-type rules frequently. Card issuers change their benefits, eligibility, and booking requirements. Regional routes, promotional fares, elite status, and codeshare agreements can all alter what you actually pay at check-in. Always verify the final cost on the airline's own website (and your card's terms) before booking.

We maintain the database from public sources and refresh it regularly, and every entry shows a "Verified MMM YYYY" timestamp in the tooltip. You can report a wrong fee from the tooltip's Notes tab. But we cannot guarantee every fee is correct at every moment for every fare class on every route.
