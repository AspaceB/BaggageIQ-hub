Pro · optional

Your wallet, decoded.

Travel cards give you free bags. But only on certain airlines, only for certain cabins, only if you book on that specific card. BaggageIQ knows all the rules — and bakes them into the real price on every flight.

  • 62 travel cards, 80 benefits. Delta SkyMiles Amex, United Explorer Chase, British Airways Amex, Citi AAdvantage — and more, refreshed weekly.
  • Every coverage rule. Primary only vs primary + companions. Domestic only vs any route. "If booked on card" caveats stamped inline.
  • Seat selection fees. Real seat fees for 109 airlines — standard, preferred, extra legroom, exit row. Choose your seat preference and see the true total.
  • Card seat benefits. 15 cards cover seat selection fees — Delta Reserve gives Comfort+, Amex Platinum's $200 credit covers seat fees, Citi Executive gets Main Cabin Extra free.
  • Lives on your device. No server ever sees which cards are in your wallet.
