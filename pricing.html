Questions · answered

Short, honest answers about Pro, refunds, and how it all works.

Is the free tier really free forever?

Yes. The baggage overlay, scoring, 109-airline database, and 5 site parsers are free with no time limit, no account, no credit card. That will never change.

What exactly does Pro unlock?

One thing: credit-card baggage benefits. Add your travel cards to the popup wallet and BaggageIQ subtracts their free-bag allowances from the real price and score on every flight. If your Delta SkyMiles Amex gives you 1 free checked bag on Delta, a Delta flight that shows +$70 bags to everyone else will show bags free to you.

One-time or subscription?

One-time payment, lifetime access. Pay $14 once, use Pro forever on the Chrome profile where you activate it. No recurring charges, no expiring licences, no credit card on file.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — 14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Email support@baggageiq.app and we'll process the refund through Paddle. This applies to everyone globally; EU/UK statutory withdrawal rights are covered under this same window.

How does Paddle work?

Paddle is our merchant of record — they handle the payment, the invoice, VAT collection (for EU / UK / global tax compliance), and refund processing. Your card details never touch BaggageIQ; we only receive confirmation that you paid, along with a licence token. See Paddle's Buyer Terms for their side.

Will BaggageIQ become an ad-funded / affiliate product?

No. There are no ads, no affiliate links, no tracking pixels, and no data sales in the codebase. The free tier is funded by the one-time Pro purchases. If that stops working, we'll be honest about it — we won't quietly start selling data.

Will my Pro licence work on multiple devices?

Yes, across every Chrome profile where you're signed in — the licence state is stored in chrome.storage.sync, which Chrome itself syncs between your devices. One purchase covers every device logged into the same Chrome profile.

What if the card benefit is wrong and I get charged at check-in?

Card benefits change, fare classes have exceptions, and "if booked on this card" rules are easy to miss. BaggageIQ shows the coverage details and caveats inline, but always verify in your card's terms. See the accuracy disclaimer in the Terms — fees and card benefits are estimates, not guarantees.

How do I upgrade my existing extension to Pro?

After you complete checkout with Paddle, you'll receive an email with a licence token (format BIQ-PRO-...). Open the BaggageIQ extension popup, scroll to the BaggageIQ Pro section, paste your token, and click Activate. Your wallet of travel cards and all Pro features become available immediately.
